§ 03 — What it does

Three things. Nothing else.

No dashboards you don't use. No features that exist only on the pricing page. Three clear jobs — done well.

I · For Reps

Feedback, in the same hour as the call.

Short, specific, tied to real moments in the transcript. No waiting for a weekly 1:1. No guessing what "be more consultative" means.

II · For Managers

Coach on behavior, not outcomes.

See what your team is actually doing across discovery, demos, and closes. Trigger targeted coaching in a click — without scrubbing through hours of recordings.

III · For Leaders

Training that finally sticks.

Every rep, every week, measured against the methodology you invested in. Drift becomes visible the day it starts — not the quarter it shows up in the number.
