Most sales training follows the same arc.

Week 1: Reps are energized. New frameworks, new language, new confidence.

Week 4: They're using parts of it. The easy parts.

Week 12: They've drifted back to what they were doing before. The training binder is on a shelf. The methodology is a memory.

The problem isn't the training. It's the silence after it.

Reinforcement isn't a nice-to-have. It's the difference between a methodology your team knows and one they actually use.
