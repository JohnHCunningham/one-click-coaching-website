## Deal Flow Assumptions

### Channel 1: Cold Email
**Volume:** 250 emails sent over 90 days
**Funnel:**
- Open rate: 40% = 100 opens
- Response rate: 8% = 20 responses
- Discovery calls: 50% of responses = 10 calls
- Demo conversion: 60% = 6 demos
- Close rate: 33% = **2 deals**

**Timeline:** First deal Week 6-8, second deal Week 10-12

### Channel 3: LinkedIn Content
**Volume:** 24 posts over 90 days (2/week)

- Inbound traffic → 3-5 discovery calls
