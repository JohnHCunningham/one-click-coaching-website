TITLE: OCC Rep Walkthrough — Notes & Coaching in 60 Seconds
DATE: June 9, 2026

CORE IDEA: A new rep logs into OCC. Here's where to find coaching after every call and how to use Notes to stay connected with their manager. Two features, one simple workflow.

SCRIPT DIRECTION:

HOOK (5s): "Your manager invited you to One Click Coaching. Here's what to do now."

NOTES (20s): "First — Notes. This is your direct line to your manager. They can send you coaching follow-ups after calls. But you can also start the conversation — ask a question, flag a deal, share a win. It's private, just between you and your manager. No email chains. No Slack messages getting buried. One place for the conversations that matter."

COACHING (25s): "After every call, OCC scores how you executed the methodology. You'll see a scorecard — green means you nailed it, terracotta means there's room to grow. Tap into any call to see exactly what went well and where to focus next. This isn't a report card. It's a coach that remembers every call and tells you what to practice."

CLOSE (5s): "Check Notes after every 1-on-1. Check Coaching after every call. That's the rhythm."

WORD COUNT TARGET: 150-160 words
DURATION TARGET: 55-60 seconds
NO CTA. The platform speaks for itself.
