# Warm Lead Pipeline — Middle Funnel Tracker

Tracks connections from "accepted" to "discovery call." Only active warm relationships. Not the full Notes tab — just people who are moving.

## Stage Definitions

| Stage | Signal | Action | Timing |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Stage 1 — Connected** | Accepted your request | Thank-you note | Day 0 |
| **Stage 2 — Engaged** | They replied, commented on your post, or viewed your profile again | One question about their world | Day 1-3 |
| **Stage 3 — Warm** | They replied substantively (more than "you too" or "glad to connect") | Soft discovery invite | Day 5-7 |
| **Stage 4 — Booked** | Discovery call scheduled | Prep doc, show up | — |
| **Stage 5 — Demo/Proposal** | Call completed, they want to see OCC | Walkthrough or proposal | — |

## Stage 3 Invite Template

Use when they've replied substantively:

"[Name] — appreciated that insight on [their challenge]. If you've got 20 minutes sometime, I'd love to compare notes on [specific topic they raised]. No pitch — just curious how you're handling it. Here's my calendar: https://tidycal.com/aiautomations/30-minute-meeting"

## Sunday Audit Checklist

- [ ] Who moved from Stage 1 → Stage 2 this week?
- [ ] Who's been at Stage 2 for 2+ weeks with no movement?
- [ ] Who hit Stage 3 and needs an invite?
- [ ] Any Stage 2 who viewed your profile this week? → Move to Stage 3 invite
- [ ] Any discovery calls to prep for this week?
- [ ] Any demos/proposals to follow up on?

## Rules

- Never invite to discovery from Stage 1. They haven't earned it.
- Profile view after Stage 2 = automatic Stage 3 eligibility.
- Two weeks at Stage 2 with no movement = send one light touch. Then leave them.
- The invite is ALWAYS "compare notes" — never "show you OCC."
