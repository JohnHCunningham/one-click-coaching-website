# Profile Viewers — Tracking Worksheet

## How It Works

1. John checks "Who viewed my profile" on LinkedIn
2. Sends viewer details to Hermes → logged here with category
3. Three categories:
   - **Pending** — hasn't accepted invite. Wait 5 days. Send "what caught your eye" email.
   - **Follow** — influencer/creator. Good sources for commenting. No email.
   - **Message** — already connected. Can DM "what caught your eye?" directly.
4. Pending viewers mature after 5 days → Hermes drafts email → John approves → send
