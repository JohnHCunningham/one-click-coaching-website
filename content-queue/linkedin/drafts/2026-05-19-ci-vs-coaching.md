## Draft

Conversation intelligence tells you what happened.  
Coaching changes what happens next.

Your team already has call recordings. Transcripts. Talk ratios. Keyword tracking.  
All useful.  
But none of it is the same as methodology reinforcement.

A manager knowing a rep talked too much doesn't mean the rep learns how to run a better Pain Funnel.  
Seeing "discovery" in the CRM stage doesn't mean the upfront contract was actually set.  
Having a dashboard doesn't mean the methodology survived the call.

The gap between seeing the problem and fixing the behavior is where training ROI disappears.

Conversation intelligence shows you the drift.  
Coaching stops it.
