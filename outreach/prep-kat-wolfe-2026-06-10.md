## Questions (Sandler-Style — Pain First, Solution Never)

### Open with her frame
> "You said 'systems problem, not talent problem.' That jumped out at me because it's exactly what I see. What does that look like in the organizations you work with?"

### Drill into the gap
1. "When you do a GTM engagement or methodology training with a client — Challenger, SPIN, GAP — what happens 60-90 days after you leave?"
2. "How do your clients reinforce the methodology between your sessions?"
3. "Do you ever hear from clients that the training was great but the reps 'drifted back'?"

### Find the cost
4. "What does that drift cost them — in pipeline, in forecast accuracy, in rep ramp time?"
5. "Have you ever lost a renewal or follow-on engagement because the first one didn't stick?"

### Test the partnership angle
6. "If you had a way to score every rep call against the methodology — automatically, call by call — and surface exactly where each rep is drifting, would that extend the value of what you already deliver?"
7. "What would it mean for your business if you could offer clients a reinforcement layer under the KRATOS brand?"
