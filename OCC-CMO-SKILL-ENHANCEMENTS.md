## Enhancement #1: Rhetorical Devices (Added to outreach.md)

### Location
`references/outreach.md` - Lines 17-59

### What Was Added
- **Mandatory requirement:** Every cold email must include 2-4 rhetorical devices
- **The 6 most effective devices for emails:**
  1. Antithesis (contrasting ideas)
  2. Tricolon (rule of three)
  3. Anaphora (repetition at beginning)
  4. Metaphor (direct comparison)
  5. Rhetorical Question (use sparingly)
  6. Anadiplosis (linking sentences)
- **Examples of each device in email context**
- **Usage guidelines:** Minimum 2, optimal 3-4, maximum 5 per email

### Why This Matters
Makes cold emails memorable and persuasive, standing out from generic outreach. Same devices you use in blog posts and LinkedIn now applied to emails.
