# InMail — Voice Rules

- Subject line: create curiosity without being clickbait
- 400 characters max
- Open with the problem they have, not who you are
- One question
- No links in first message
- Voice: peer to peer, not vendor to buyer

# Filter Rules

SEND if: VP Sales, Sales Manager, Sales Director, Sales Enablement, CRO, Head of Sales.
Team size does not matter.
SKIP if: no sales leadership role, solo founder, individual contributor.
