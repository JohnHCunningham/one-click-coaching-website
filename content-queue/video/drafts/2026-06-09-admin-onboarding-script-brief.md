TITLE: OCC Admin Onboarding — 60 Second Walkthrough
DATE: June 9, 2026

CORE IDEA: New admins see 6 setup steps on their dashboard. This video walks through the three that unlock the most value fastest — Goals & Targets, Settings, and Integrations — and explains what each one does for the team.

SCRIPT DIRECTION:

HOOK (5s): "You just signed up for One Click Coaching. Here's what to do first — in any order."

GOALS & TARGETS (15s): "Set rep targets for activity, discovery, and quota. This is how the system knows what 'good' looks like for your team. Without goals, coaching is generic. With goals, every scorecard and celebration is personal to your reps."

SETTINGS (15s): "Under Settings, add your company context — who you sell to, what pain you solve, who you compete against. The more the system knows about your world, the smarter the coaching becomes. It's not generic advice. It's advice that knows your buyer."

INTEGRATIONS (15s): "Connect Fathom or HubSpot. This is where calls start flowing in. One connection, and every rep's calls get scored against your methodology. No rep setup required — you connect once, the whole team benefits."

CLOSE (5s): "Six steps. Any order. Each one makes the coaching sharper. Start wherever you want."

WORD COUNT TARGET: 150-160 words
DURATION TARGET: 55-60 seconds
NO CTA. The dashboard already has the buttons.
