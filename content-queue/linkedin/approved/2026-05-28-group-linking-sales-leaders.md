# LSL Group Post — Enablement ROI Blind Spot (Agnostic)

**Group:** Linking Sales Leaders
**Day:** Wednesday
**Angle:** The measurement gap — methodology-agnostic
**Round:** 2 (mechanism)

---

Your team finished sales training.

They were energized. They certified. The workshop closed with commitments.

Thirty days later, you cannot prove a single rep is still using the methodology on live calls.

You have attendance data. You have NPS scores. You have certification results. You have anecdotal feedback — "reps said it was great."

What you do not have is behavior data. Call-by-call proof that the methodology showed up when pressure arrived. That the Up-Front Contract was set. That discovery went deep. That budget was actually qualified.

This is the measurement gap most sales leaders never name.

CRM stages tell you where a deal is. They do not tell you whether it was qualified the way your team was trained to qualify.

The question is not whether the training was good. It is whether you can prove it changed behavior.

When was the last time you could point to data showing your methodology is still being executed, call by call, 90 days later?
