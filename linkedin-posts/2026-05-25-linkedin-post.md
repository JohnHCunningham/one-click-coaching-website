## Post Content

87% of new sales skills disappear within 30 days.

Most organizations solve this with more training. More sessions, more content, more pressure.

But neuroscience reveals the real culprit: the brain's default mode network actively erases new behaviors that aren't immediately rewarded.

Your reps aren't forgetting the new objection handling technique because they don't care. They're forgetting because their neural pathways are literally pruning unused connections every night during sleep.

The solution isn't more training—it's training more strategically.

Successful reinforcement requires three elements: spaced repetition, contextual practice, and immediate application feedback. Not next month. Not next week. Within 24 hours of learning.

We don't need to train harder. We need to train smarter.

What's your experience with skill retention after training programs?
