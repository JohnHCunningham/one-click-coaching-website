# Challenger Group Post — Enablement ROI Blind Spot (Challenger adaptation)

**Group:** The Challenger Sale
**Day:** Thursday
**Angle:** The measurement gap — Challenger-specific
**Round:** 2 (mechanism)

---

Your team learned to teach, tailor, and take control.

They certified. They role-played commercial teaching. They built reframes.

Thirty days later, commercial teaching is becoming solution selling. Tailoring is becoming templating. Taking control is becoming hoping the deal advances.

And you cannot prove any of it is happening.

Because what's measured after training is attendance, satisfaction, and certification — not whether a rep actually led with insight, challenged the customer's thinking, or reframed the problem on a live call Tuesday morning.

The CRM says the deal moved to proposal. The call says the rep never taught anything new.

That gap — between what training promised and what's actually showing up on calls — is invisible to most sales leaders. Not because they don't care. Because the tools to measure methodology execution at scale haven't existed.

What's been your experience with the gap between Challenger training and what actually survives live calls?
