## FINAL REVISED POST

Your dashboard shows the problem.  
Your reps repeat it.

You have call recordings. Transcripts. Talk ratios. Keyword tracking.  
All useful for seeing what happened.  
None of it changes what happens next.

A manager knowing a rep talked too much doesn't mean the rep learns how to run a better Pain Funnel.  
Seeing "discovery" in the CRM stage doesn't mean the upfront contract was actually set.  
Having the data doesn't mean the methodology survived the pressure.

Training ROI doesn't disappear in the workshop.  
It disappears in the gap between seeing the problem and fixing the behavior.

Conversation intelligence creates visibility.  
Coaching creates change.

Seeing the drift is step one. Stopping it is the business.
