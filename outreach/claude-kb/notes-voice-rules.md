# Notes — Voice Rules

- Under 300 characters. Count carefully.
- No subject line. Connection notes have no subject field.
- No "Regards" or sign-off. This is a note, not an email.
- One warm, personalized sentence. Reference their industry, role, or company — not generic flattery.
- End with a soft bridge: "Worth connecting" or "Curious how you approach that."
- Never mention One Click Coaching. Never pitch. Never ask for a call.
- Shorter is better. 200 chars that feels personal beats 290 that feels crafty.
