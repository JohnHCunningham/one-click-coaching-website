Chapter 02 — 04 Logo directions

directions to choose from.

§ Direction A
005

The Pulse.
A rhythmic mark.

A slender circular contour with a single offset beat — the “one” in One Click. Evokes cadence, heartbeat, signal. It's the sound of a sales call going well, reduced to its simplest shape.

Chapter 05 The visual system
The

system — color & type.

Shared across all three directions. The system is designed to feel like an editorial property — closer to The Atlantic or Monocle than a SaaS dashboard. The color is warm. The type is measured. Nothing glows.
