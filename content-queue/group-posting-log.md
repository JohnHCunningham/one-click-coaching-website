## Posting Rules

- 1 profile post per day (Tue/Wed/Thu) → goes to Buffer
- 1 group post per day (Tue/Wed/Thu) → goes to day's designated group
- Tuesday: group post is exact copy of profile post
- Wednesday: group post is Sandler-modified version of profile post
- Thursday: group post is Challenger-modified version of profile post
- 3 profile posts + 3 group posts = 6 posts/week total
- Cron drafts at 9 AM — John reviews and posts manually
