# Independent Sales Trainer Partner ICP

Last updated: June 8, 2026

## Profile

**Methodology-first:** Teaches a named framework — Challenger, SPIN, Gap Selling, MEDDIC, ValueSelling, Command of the Message. Not a generic "sales coach." They've hung their shingle on a specific system.

**Established, not starting out:** 3+ years in business. Stable client base of B2B companies. They're not hunting for their next meal — they're looking for leverage and recurring revenue.

**Solo or small team (1-5):** No internal enablement department. They ARE the product. This matters because they feel the capacity ceiling — only so many hours they can bill. A reinforcement layer gives them revenue between engagements.

**Knows the drift problem:** They've trained companies that regressed. They've had the awkward conversation: "Your team isn't using it." They already sell against drift — we just give them the diagnostics to prove it and fix it.

**Client overlap with OCC ICP:** Their clients are B2B companies with 5-50 reps. VP Sales / CRO buyers. Same pond.

**LinkedIn-active:** Posts about methodology, coaching, sales leadership. You can see their thinking before you reach out. Warm-in through content engagement.

## The Wedge

"You install the operating system. We built the diagnostics and reinforcement layer. White-label, your brand, recurring revenue for you. One pilot client, 30 days."

## Search Path (Sales Nav)

- Keywords: "sales trainer" OR "sales coach" + "Challenger" OR "SPIN" OR "MEDDIC" OR "Gap Selling"
- Geography: Ontario/Canada
- Connections: 500+
- Look for: owner/operator, founder, independent (not employed by a training firm)

## Anti-ICP (Skip These)

- Internal sales enablement at a company (they don't resell)
- Large training firms (FranklinCovey, Richardson, etc. — too slow, procurement hell)
- Trainers who only do keynotes/events (no ongoing client relationships)
- New trainers (< 1 year, no book of business)
- Trainers with no named methodology (generic "sales coaching")
