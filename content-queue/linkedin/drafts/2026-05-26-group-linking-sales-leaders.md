# LinkedIn Group Post Draft
## Date: 2026-05-26 (Tuesday)
## Group: Linking Sales Leaders
## Methodology: Agnostic
## Angle: Manager bandwidth — they know but can't reinforce
## Round: 1 (name the problem, no product pitch)

---

They know what good looks like.

Your sales managers can spot a skipped step from across the room.
They've done the training. They've run the deals.
They know exactly where the methodology broke down.

They just can't reinforce it fast enough.

Not because they don't care.
Not because the training was wrong.
Not because the rep isn't capable.

But because one manager covering eight reps can't sit in on every call.

The gap isn't knowledge.

It's bandwidth.
