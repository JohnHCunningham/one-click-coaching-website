## Rules (from LinkedIn Operating Map)

- Phase 1 (Week 1): Thank-you only. Let them see your content.
- Phase 2 (Weeks 1-4): Your posts do the warming. Watch for theirs.
- Phase 3: When they post in your wheelhouse → 3-part comment protocol.
- Phase 4 (Week 4+): Only after 2-3 comment exchanges, consider a DM.
- Never: second DM that's a pitch. Never: "tell me about yourself."
