## WHAT OCC IS

**The mechanism (in order):**
1. Rep goes on a live call. Pressure arrives. They revert to old patterns.
2. OCC scores the call automatically. No rep input. No new workflow.
3. Rep sees an accurate mirror of what actually happened. This is when permanent change begins.
4. Coaching delivered same-hour. Fast enough to rewire the pattern.
5. System remembers. Every call, every rep. Gets smarter over time.

**The Mirror Effect:** We behave differently when we can see ourselves clearly. Accurate self-observation is the catalyst for permanent behaviour change. Not accountability. Not consequence. The mirror.

**The Execution Loop™:** The four-step cycle OCC runs — Track → Review → Adjust → Apply. Trademarked. The "what it is."

**[X] Protocol:** Two-word coined name, second word is "Protocol." Created May 2026. **NOT YET CAPTURED — ask John.**

**What it is not:** Not Gong. Not a recording tool. Not training. Not a CRM. The instrumentation layer that makes the methodology the team already paid for actually show up on calls.

**One sentence:** Permanent behaviour change under pressure — through transparency, immediate feedback, and a system that gets smarter every time it's used.
