# LinkedIn Posts — Voice Rules (Profile + Group)

- One idea
- Short lines
- 12-25 lines total
- Open with a provocation
- Close with a question
- Maximum 3 hashtags
- No emojis except — (em dash)
- No "Here's why..."
- Voice: someone who's been in the room, not someone who read about the room

# Group Posts — Additional Rule

- Add one specific question that invites debate
- End with "Curious what others are seeing." or similar
- No selling
- No links in the post body
- Comment with a link only if it adds direct value
