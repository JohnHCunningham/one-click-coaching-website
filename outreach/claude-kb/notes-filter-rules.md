# Notes — Filter Rules (OVERRIDES ALL PREVIOUS)

SEND if the person holds: VP Sales, Sales Manager, Sales Director, Sales Enablement, CRO, Head of Sales.

Team size does NOT matter. The signal is the role:
- VP of Sales with 3 reps = SEND
- Sales Manager with 53 reps = SEND
- Sales Director at a 200-person company = SEND

SKIP only if:
- No dedicated sales leader (solo founder)
- Individual contributor (AE, BDR, SDR — no management title)
- Company has no sales team at all
- Sales Xceleration / fractional VP (sells VP-as-a-service, no W2 reps)
- Real estate brokerages, CPG/retail, media ad sales, hospitality
- Active bankruptcy
- Duplicate in this batch
