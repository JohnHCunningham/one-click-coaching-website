BLOG: "Why Coaching ROI Beats Hiring ROI Every Time"
DATE: June 9, 2026
SLUG: coaching-roi-vs-hiring-roi-sales-teams

CORE IDEA: Most sales leaders default to hiring when the pipeline grows. But improving one existing mid-pack rep by 20% delivers better ROI — no recruiting cost, no ramp time, no pipeline gap. The math nobody runs.

KEY ARGUMENT: A new hire costs $45K fully loaded before they book a single qualified meeting. An existing rep already knows the product, already has pipeline. The only thing missing is consistent methodology execution — and that is coachable. One rep, 20% improvement, compounding every quarter.

THE INVISIBLE COST: Nobody tracks what methodology drift costs. Deals that almost close. Forecasts that almost hold. Pipeline that almost converts. The CRM is green. The call says the rep never qualified budget. Make it visible and the math changes.

CLOSE: When the headcount conversation starts, ask a different question — not "how many reps do we need to add?" but "how many reps do we already have who are almost executing the methodology?"
