# LinkedIn Post 2 — CRM vs Methodology
# Schedule: Tue Jun 2, 8:00 AM ET
# Pillar: Methodology Execution / Cost of Drift
# Source: Research brief angle 2

The CRM says the stage changed.

The call says the methodology disappeared.

You have seen this.

A deal moves from Discovery to Demo.
The rep logged the meeting.
Attached a deck.
Updated the stage.

The CRM looks clean.

But on the call, the rep skipped the upfront contract.
Never asked a real pain question.
Rushed into features the buyer did not ask for.

The stage moved.
The methodology did not.

CRM hygiene creates a dangerous false positive.
It rewards activity.
But methodology adoption is not a field in the CRM.

It is a behavior on the call.

And behavior, unchecked, drifts toward whatever is easiest in the moment — not whatever was taught in the workshop.

The question is not whether your team uses the CRM.

It is whether the CRM reflects buyer commitment or rep optimism.
