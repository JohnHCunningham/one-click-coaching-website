# LinkedIn Post 3 — Conversation Intelligence Is Not Coaching
# Schedule: Wed Jun 3, 12:00 PM ET
# Pillar: Reinforcement Loop / Methodology Execution
# Source: Research brief angle 3

Conversation intelligence tells you what happened.

Coaching changes what happens next.

They are not the same thing.

A transcript can show a rep talked 67% of the call.

It cannot, by itself, teach that rep to ask better pain questions next time.

A dashboard can flag a low talk-to-listen ratio.

It cannot reinforce the Sandler upfront contract or the Gap Selling business case or the MEDDIC champion check.

Those are methodology behaviors.
Not generic communication habits.

The category confusion is understandable.
Every platform now claims AI coaching.
But most are still describing the call — not correcting the behavior.

Describing the call is useful.

Correcting the behavior against a trained methodology — that is the reinforcement loop most teams are missing.
