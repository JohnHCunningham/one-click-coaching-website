# Partners — Voice Rules

- Find their email first (format: first.last@sandler.com for Sandler)
- Connection note is fallback (300 char limit)
- Open with shared context: "We both serve sales leaders — you through methodology, me through reinforcement"
- Frame as complementary, not competitive
- One call to action: 20-minute call
- Never ask to "pick their brain"

# Filter Rules

SEND if: VP Sales, Sales Manager, Sales Director, Sales Enablement, CRO, Head of Sales.
For Sandler specifically: franchisees and corporate trainers who lead sales teams.
Team size does not matter.
SKIP if: no sales leadership role.
