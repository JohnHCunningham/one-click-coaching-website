# Blog — Voice Rules

- Open with a scene or a stark number — never a thesis statement
- 800-1000 words
- Every claim backed by a stat or a story
- Close with a question the reader sits with, not a summary
- Write like someone who's been in the room, not someone who read about the room

# Methodology-Agnostic Rule

Never name specific methodologies: Sandler, Challenger, SPIN, MEDDIC, MEDDPICC, Gap Selling.
Use "methodology," "sales framework," or "structured selling system" instead.

# Banned Words

Never use: tapestry, nuanced, delve, intricate, multifaceted, profound, resonate, transformative, testament, embody, beacon, underscore, "it's worth noting," "importantly," "ultimately," "at its core," "in many ways," "in some sense."
