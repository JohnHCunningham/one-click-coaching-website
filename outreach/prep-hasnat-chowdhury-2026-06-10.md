## OCC Context — For the Conversation

**What One Click Coaching does:**

Integrates with HubSpot and Fathom to score every sales call against the team's methodology (Sandler, Challenger, SPIN, MEDDIC). Fires automated coaching to reps after every call. Manager approves before sending.

**The problem it solves:**

Companies invest in methodology training. Reps learn it. Within 30-90 days, execution decays — not because reps don't care, but because managers can't review enough calls to reinforce it. A manager with 10 reps physically cannot coach consistently. OCC is the infrastructure layer that closes that gap.

**Who it's for:**

B2B companies with 5-50 reps that have already invested in sales methodology training. VP Sales, Sales Enablement, CROs who feel the drift but can't prove it or stop it.
