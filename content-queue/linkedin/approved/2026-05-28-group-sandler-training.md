# Sandler Group Post — Enablement ROI Blind Spot (Sandler adaptation)

**Group:** Sandler Sales Training
**Day:** Tuesday
**Angle:** The measurement gap — Sandler-specific
**Round:** 2 (mechanism)

---

Your client finishes Sandler training.

They certify. They role-play. The workshop ends with energy.

Thirty days later, the Up-Front Contract is disappearing. The Pain Funnel is getting shallow. Reversing is becoming rare.

And nobody can prove it.

Not because the client doesn't care. Because they have no way to measure whether the Sandler methodology is showing up on live calls. They track attendance. They track NPS. They track certification.

None of those tell them whether a rep used the Up-Front Contract on Tuesday morning.

This is the measurement gap that sits between every Sandler training engagement and every renewal conversation. Your client cannot prove the training worked — not at the level that matters. And when renewal time comes, the conversation is about faith, not evidence.

What would change if you could show a client their team's Sandler execution score — call by call, rep by rep, trended over 90 days?

That conversation is different.

What's been your experience with clients who finish training but can't see whether it's sticking?
