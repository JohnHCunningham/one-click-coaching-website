Questions, answered.

How is a seat counted?
A seat is any active rep or manager who logs into the platform in a given month. Add or remove seats any time — we prorate the change to the day.
Is there a minimum team size?
No minimum. You can start with a single rep and scale up. There's no per-team floor, no setup fee, no onboarding charge.
What about annual billing?
Annual commits get two months free — effectively $500/rep/year instead of $600. Contact us for enterprise multi-year pricing.
Can we try it first?
Yes — 30-day free trial with no credit card. If you're not seeing execution lift, cancel with one click. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee after you've paid.
What about 50+ rep teams?
Teams over 50 seats get volume pricing, a dedicated success manager, custom integrations, white-label options, and SLA guarantees. Talk to us.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your data stays accessible for 30 days after cancellation so you can export everything.
