# Independent Trainer Partner — Knowledge Base

## One Click Coaching — What We Are
OCC is an AI sales coaching platform that scores every call against the client's sales methodology and surfaces rep-specific reinforcement. We don't teach methodology — we make methodology stick after training. The core insight: training creates awareness. Reinforcement builds automaticity. Without call-by-call scoring, methodology erodes within weeks of training.

## The Partner Model
White-label OCC under the trainer's brand. Trainer offers it to their existing clients as a reinforcement layer. Recurring revenue for the trainer. OCC handles the tech — call scoring, dashboards, rep-specific coaching prompts. The trainer owns the relationship, the methodology, and the brand.

## The Pilot Offer
One client, 30 days, under the trainer's brand, no cost to the trainer or the client. Prove the reinforcement works. Then discuss commercial terms.

## ICP — Who We Want
- Teaches a named methodology (Challenger, SPIN, Gap Selling, MEDDIC, ValueSelling, Command of the Message, Great Demo, BridgeSelling, etc.)
- 3+ years in business with stable B2B client base
- Solo or small team (1-5 people)
- LinkedIn-active — posts about methodology, coaching, sales leadership
- Client overlap: B2B companies with 5-50 reps, VP Sales / CRO buyers

## Anti-ICP — Who We Skip
- Internal sales enablement at a company
- Large training firms (FranklinCovey, Richardson, etc.)
- Keynote-only speakers
- Trainers without a named methodology
- Trainers with < 1 year in business
- Non-sales trainers (leadership coaches, EOS implementers, health coaches)
