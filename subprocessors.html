Sub-processors.

We use a small number of trusted third parties to help us deliver One Click Coaching. Here's the complete list.

Last Updated
January 1, 2025

Sub-processors are third-party service providers we use to help operate our service. All sub-processors are contractually required to maintain the security and confidentiality of your data.

Vercel
vercel.com
Infrastructure and hosting platform. Provides compute, edge functions, and CDN services. Data stored in US and global edge locations.
Infrastructure
Supabase
supabase.com
Database and authentication services. Stores user accounts, team configuration, and call analysis data. Data encrypted at rest with AES-256.
Database
Anthropic
anthropic.com
AI analysis engine. Processes call transcripts to generate coaching insights. Does not train models on customer data. Data transmitted via encrypted API.
AI Processing
Stripe
stripe.com
Payment processing. Handles billing, subscriptions, and payment methods. We never store credit card numbers—Stripe handles all payment data.
Payments
Resend
resend.com
Transactional email delivery. Sends account notifications, coaching alerts, and system emails. Does not access call data or analysis results.
Email
Formspree
formspree.io
Contact form processing. Receives and forwards contact form submissions from our website. Does not access customer data in the application.
Forms

Changes to this list.

We'll update this page when we add or remove sub-processors. Material changes will be announced via email at least 30 days in advance.
