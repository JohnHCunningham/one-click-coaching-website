Cookie Policy.

We use a minimal number of cookies to make the service work. Here's what they do.

Last Updated

§ 01

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

We use cookies and similar technologies (like local storage) to provide and improve our service.

§ 02

Cookies we use.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the service to function:

  • Authentication — Keeps you logged in securely
  • Session management — Maintains your session state
  • Security — Protects against CSRF attacks

These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Analytics Cookies

We use Vercel Analytics to understand how people use our service:

  • Page views — Which pages are visited
  • Feature usage — Which features are used most
  • Performance metrics — Page load times and errors

This data is anonymized and aggregated. We don't track individual users across websites.

Cookies We Don't Use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Cross-site tracking
§ 03

Managing cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies — Note that this will prevent you from logging in
  • Delete existing cookies — This will log you out
  • Block third-party cookies — Recommended and won't affect functionality

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.

You can opt out of analytics cookies from your account settings without affecting core functionality.

§ 04

Changes to this policy.

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
